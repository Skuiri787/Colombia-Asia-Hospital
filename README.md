# 🏥 Columbia Asia Hospital Analytics Project

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing hospital operational and patient data using **SQL, Power BI, and Data Analytics techniques** to generate actionable business insights for hospital management.

The objective was to evaluate hospital performance, patient behavior, departmental efficiency, revenue generation, and operational bottlenecks while providing strategic recommendations for improving patient satisfaction and business outcomes.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze hospital revenue generation
- Identify departments suitable for new doctor hiring
- Design data-driven patient discount strategies
- Evaluate patient satisfaction and waiting times
- Monitor department and doctor performance
- Support management decision-making through interactive dashboards

## 💡 Key Findings

- Orthopedics generated the highest revenue.
- General Practice and Orthopedics contributed nearly 66% of total revenue.
- Patients aged 36–60 represented the largest patient segment.
- Neurology reported the highest average waiting time.
- Patient satisfaction showed a mild decline as waiting time increased.
- Revenue and patient visits exhibited a gradual downward trend.
- Gender distribution remained balanced across most doctors.

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

- Increase staffing in high-demand departments.
- Implement appointment scheduling and queue management.
- Introduce a two-shift model for General Practice.
- Use targeted discount strategies instead of blanket discounts.
- Monitor patient satisfaction and operational KPIs regularly.
- Focus growth initiatives on high-revenue departments.
